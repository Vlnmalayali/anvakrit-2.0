About NFSU

The National Forensic Sciences University, an Institution of National Importance under the Ministry of Home Affairs, Government of India, is established by the Government of India Act, 2020. NFSU encompasses forensics, engineering, technology, life sciences, behavioral science, clinical and forensic psychology, law, pharmacy, cyber security, digital forensics, management, forensic accounting and interdisciplinary areas.

About Anvakrit 2.0

After the resounding success of its first edition, Anvakrit 2.0 returns from 11th-13th February 2026 with greater depth, tougher challenges, and a bold new vision. Centered around the theme of Digital Forensics, the competition invites participants to step Beyond the Crime Scene and test their investigative acumen in dynamic, real-world scenarios.

Anvakrit 2.0 blends traditional forensics with modern digital investigation—featuring DNA analysis, chemical trace evidence, fingerprint patterns, cyber trails, and legal frameworks. This is where science meets law, technology meets investigation, and theory meets practice.

Competition Structure

Navigate through multiple rounds combining digital and physical forensics

Category I - Undergraduate Track

1

Round 1: Case File Analysis

The journey begins with a case file (online/offline) to test investigative, critical thinking and deductive skills. Teams must analyze physical evidence, digital traces, and forensic patterns. Examine DNA samples, chemical residues, digital footprints, and legal documentation within the given time limit.

2

Round 2: Forensic Poster Presentation

Online poster presentation showcasing case file findings and methods used. Integrate physical forensics (fingerprints, DNA), chemical analysis, digital evidence, and legal reasoning. Most creative posters with accurate solutions advance to Round 3. Top teams based on cumulative scores of Rounds 1 & 2 qualify for the offline round.

3

Round 3: Offline CSI Challenge

Top teams advance to offline round at NFSU Delhi Campus for hands-on crime scene investigation, forensic workshops covering DNA analysis, chemical testing, digital forensics tools, fingerprint techniques, and lab visits to experience real-world forensic science combining traditional and modern methods.

Category II - Postgraduate Track

1

Round 1: Advanced Case File

CLASSIFIED: Throughout the competition, from the day registration starts, various surprise puzzles and clues shall be shared. Offline case file to test investigative, critical thinking and deductive skills across multiple forensic domains—biological, chemical, digital, and legal. Teams must respond accurately within the time limit. Only top teams who responded correctly advance on first-come, first-serve basis.

2

Round 2: Integrated Crime Scene Investigation

Multi-layered, domain integrated immersive crime scene investigation combining:

  • Physical evidence collection (DNA, fingerprints, trace materials)
  • Chemical & toxicological analysis
  • Digital forensics & cyber trail recovery
  • Psychological profiling & behavioral evidence
  • Mini-lab testing with real equipment
  • Chain of custody documentation
3

Round 3: Courtroom Presentation

Face a live jury in a courtroom setting. Present findings integrating physical, chemical, digital, and behavioral evidence. Defend evidence collection process, forensic methodology, and conclusions. Tests legal knowledge, scientific reasoning, and presence of mind in a professional legal environment.
